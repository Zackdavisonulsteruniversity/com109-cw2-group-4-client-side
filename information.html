Costs & Membership Options

Student Standard

£19/mo

Unlimited Belfast Campus access, weekday classes included, no guest passes.

Student Plus

£29/mo

Access to all gyms, priority class booking, 2 guest passes per month, and body-composition scan.

Staff Membership

£24/mo

All-campus access, off-peak classes included, discounted personal training sessions.
